About the app

Arkham Investigator is a fan-made companion app for Arkham Horror: The Card Game. It helps you track progress, manage chaos bag and campaign resources, and keep your investigator sheet digital — so you can play without shuffling physical tokens and cards. This site hosts the data (cards, investigators, chaos odds) that the app can load.

How to add data in the app

  1. Go to the first screen
  2. Tap the yellow icon in the top-right corner
  3. Paste the URL above into the text field (do not open it in the browser)
  4. Tap the confirmation button
  5. Enjoy!

This instruction is intended for iOS. On Android, the flow may differ slightly.
